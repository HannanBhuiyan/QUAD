SAFETY FOCUSED PROCESSES

Quad Inspection Group has a dedicated quality manager who continuously oversees our quality programs and progressively updates to reflect best practices for On-site and off-site inspection activities. We adhere strictly to our customer’s quality and safety programs and ensure they align with all applicable government regulations. We conduct above board surveys in addition to rigorous self and third-party audits to ensure high safety standards are not just on paper but implemented in practice. Upon request, Clients can review our Quality Policy, Health & Safety Policy, Environmental Policy, Drugs and Alcohol Policy, And Company Insurance coverage, as well as our qualified Work procedures.
