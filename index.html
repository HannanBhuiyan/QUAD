WHO WE ARE

We offer a wide range of technical, engineering, and plant reliability support services with a core focus in Asset Integrity Management for Fixed and Rotating Equipment, Inspection Management, Geotechnical Services, Environment engineering, and specialized NDE. Founded in 2012, Quad Industrial Group is an Asset Integrity solutions provider focusing on the Energy and allied critical infrastructure industry. We are uniquely qualified to deliver integrity-based and other innovative services to federal, regional, and local government agencies, as well as to private industrial customers across North America and worldwide.
