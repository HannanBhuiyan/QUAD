MEMBERSHIP / AFFILIATIONS

The COR certifies that a certified auditor has evaluated the employer’s health and safety management system and met the provincial standards’ requirements. Quad is approved through the Alberta Labor and co-signed by Alberta Association for Safety Partnerships (AASP). Annual safety process audits completed by this external auditor are implemented yearly.
https://aasp.ca/

Quad is a proud member and collaborator of the CCAB. As an indigenous company, we pride ourselves on partnerships that bring value to our local community while delivering and offering our clients the best skills and solutions. https://www.ccab.com/main/ccab_member/quad-industrial-group/

The CWB Group provides welding certification, management systems registration, and training in a comprehensive and integrated manner to the welding and joining industry. Quad welding inspectors, supervisors, and welding engineers have the related CWB certifications for Steel structures W47.1-09, W178.2, and the appropriate level of codes and standards certification/endorsement.
https://www.cwbgroup.org/

ASME/CSA standards are a daily tool for us at Quad. Our SMEs are entrenched in interpreting and applying these standards for client’s reliability and production initiatives for safety, social good, and sustainability the ASME/CSA association continually develops and sustains. We have a robust QMS program to support ever-greening and ensure we are in the front of the ever-evolving science and technology, updating our procedures and sharpening our expertise for superior service delivery.
https://www.asme.org/

NACE is recognized globally as the premier authority for corrosion control solutions offering technical training and certification programs, conferences, industry standards, reports, publications, technical journals, government relations activities, and more. Quad is an active member of NACE. The Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of Alberta (APEGA) regulates the practices of engineering and geoscience in Alberta on behalf of the Government of Alberta through the Engineering and Geoscience Professions Act. Quad is a licensed member and authorized permit holder to carry our engineering in Alberta’s Province as our primary HQ by an engaged and reliable PPMP. We deliver well-conducted engineering studies and applications and ensure we meet and exceed the criteria of quality set by engineering bodies.
https://www.apega.ca/

ABSA (Alberta Boilers Safety Association) is the pressure equipment safety authority for Alberta. ABSA administers Alberta’s pressure equipment safety programs under the Safety Codes Act and has the power to enforce pressure equipment safety as set out in the legislation. Quad is an authorized permit holder by the Alberta Boiler and Safety Authority to carry out Asset Integrity management. Our QMS process has been reviewed, audited, and approved by ABSA to ensure our inspections and engineering initiatives for Clients meet the provincial and federal code. Our inspectors are certified to the applicable In-service inspection categories: In-Service Pressure Equipment Inspector and In-Service Pressure Vessel & Pressure Piping Inspector. https://www.absa.ca/

API represents all segments of America’s oil and natural gas industry. Our more than 600 members produce, process, and distribute most of the nation’s Energy.
