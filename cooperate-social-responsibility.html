Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate social responsibility is an integral part of our company culture. We respect our stakeholders’ interests—our shareholders, employees, customers, suppliers, partners, and the communities in which we live, work, and play. That’s why we advance corporate programs openly and transparently that promote stewardship, both in our business and beyond.

Environmental

Sustainability is one of our core values. We back it up by using our expertise and innovative technologies to deliver environmental best practices that address environmental challenges, manage our footprint, and improve lives.

Socials

With our healthy inclusion and diversity program’s assistance, we recruit, develop, and retain thinkers and problem solvers who reflect our diverse and connected world. Quad Gives Back charitable and philanthropic efforts. Our employees are passionate about providing innovative solutions while making a positive difference in communities around the globe. Our programs include Inclusion & Diversity, Quad is an indigenous company, and we proactively give back to the Indigenous community and other identified minority groups.

Governance

We’re committed to the highest integrity standards in everything we do—a core value instilled in every enterprise level. We stay ahead of our customers’ needs by combining unique solutions with deep domain expertise, but we earn and maintain their trust by keeping our promises. We have strong and enforced procures for all Quad employees specific to the Code of Conduct, Standard of Ethical Conduct for Business Partners, and Corporate Governance.

Inclusion & Diversity

Great Ideas Come From Everyone: We pursue diversity, equity, equality, and inclusion in our workforce, our markets, our suppliers, and our services because we recognize optimum solutions require perspectives from many different backgrounds. We are committed to honoring each employee’s unique voice, empowering them to be their authentic selves at work. Our people are our most valued resource. In an open-minded and inclusive environment, they will shape the corporation’s future through collaboration and innovation as we strive to deliver a better world.
